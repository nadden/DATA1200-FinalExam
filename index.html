Being a first-year student at OsloMet

Welcome to my first website. Being a first-year student, transitioning from upper-secondary school or work-life to University may be hard and difficult, but it can also be a great experience. You may find it hard to find a good study technique, adapt to a new city or find the whole process overwhelming?

Don't worry! I will guide you through my first semester at OsloMet at TKD. Share my experiences, what the campus offers, study techniques and adapting to a new city and meeting new friends.

OsloMet Campuses

OsloMet has two campuses. The largest campus is located in the centre of Oslo, while the secound campus is in Kjeller, near the city of Lillestrøm

Pilestredet Campus

Pilestredet campus is surrounded by cafes for reading or meeting friends and restaurants where you can buy lunch at student-friendly prices. All the main attractions of downtown Oslo—including shopping, parks, bars and the Oslo fjord—are just a short walk or tram ride away.

More information about the campuses HERE

Kjeller Campus

Kjeller Campus is situated away from the hustle and bustle of Oslo, but it's easy to get here from the city using public transport. The fast-growing town of Lillestrøm is home to cafes, restaurants and shops, and is a 5 minute bus ride or 30 minute walk away. There's also a student dorm, Åråsen, within walking distance of campus.
